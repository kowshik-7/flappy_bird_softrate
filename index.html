About Game

Flappy Bird is a simple yet addictive mobile game where players control a small bird by tapping the screen to make it flap its wings and navigate through a series of pipes. The objective is to avoid crashing into the pipes while flying forward, aiming to achieve the highest distance possible. The game's minimalistic graphics and straightforward mechanics contribute to its challenging nature, as players must time their taps accurately to keep the bird airborne. With its easy-to-understand gameplay and competitive appeal, Flappy Bird gained immense popularity upon its release, leading to numerous imitations and discussions about its addictive qualities.
